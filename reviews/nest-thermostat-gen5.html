Nest Learning Thermostat (5th Gen)

The iconic smart thermostat that learns your habits.

The Nest Learning Thermostat is arguably the device that made smart home gadgets mainstream. Now in its 5th generation, it continues to promise not just convenience, but also energy savings by learning your schedule and preferences. But with more competition than ever, does the original still hold up?

Pros

  • Beautiful, premium design
  • Learns schedules automatically
  • Proven energy savings
  • Simple user interface

Cons

  • Expensive compared to rivals
  • Requires a C-wire for some HVAC systems

Design and Build Quality

The Nest's design is iconic. The polished metal ring and high-resolution circular display feel incredibly premium. It's available in several finishes to match your home's decor. The entire unit feels solid and well-crafted, and the click of the rotating bezel is satisfyingly tactile. It's more than a utility; it's a statement piece for your wall.

Performance and Features

The core feature, "learning," works as advertised. After about a week of manual adjustments, the Nest began to anticipate our needs, warming the house before we woke up and turning to an energy-saving "Eco" mode when we left. The Farsight feature, which shows the time or temperature when it detects you across the room, is genuinely useful. It integrates seamlessly with the Google Home ecosystem and other Nest products.

Setup and Software

Installation is straightforward for most homes, with the app providing excellent step-by-step instructions. However, if your HVAC system doesn't have a C-wire (common wire), you may need to install the included Power Connector, which can be more complex. The Google Home app is the primary way to control the Nest, and it's generally intuitive, allowing you to set schedules, view energy history, and control the temperature from anywhere.

Conclusion: Is It Worth It?

The Nest Learning Thermostat remains a top-tier choice for those who value design, simplicity, and a proven learning algorithm. While cheaper alternatives exist, none match the Nest's premium feel and effortless operation. If you're invested in the Google/Nest ecosystem and want a thermostat that you can set and forget, the Nest Learning Thermostat is still one of the best you can buy.
